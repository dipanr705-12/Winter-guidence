Important Things About Winter Season

  • Winter is the coldest season of the year.
  • In India, winter usually lasts from December to February.
  • Days are cool and nights are very cold.
  • Fog and frost are common in many regions.
  • People wear warm clothes like sweaters, jackets, caps, and gloves.
  • Hot food and drinks such as soup, tea, and milk are preferred.
  • Common health problems include cold, cough, and flu.
  • Rabi crops like wheat and mustard grow in winter.
  • Many trees shed their leaves during this season.
  • Winter festivals include Christmas, New Year, Lohri, Makar Sankranti, and Pongal.
