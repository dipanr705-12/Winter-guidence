Timing: Late October/November to December, bridging autumn (Sharad) and winter (Shishir).

Pre-winter is the season that comes between autumn and winter. During this period, the weather becomes cooler and pleasant, mornings and nights turn chilly, and people begin preparing for colder days. It is a comfortable time for festivals, outdoor activities, travel, and maintaining good health before deep winter starts.
