What is Cycling?

Cycling is a physical activity where a person rides a bicycle for transportation, fitness, or recreation. It is suitable for all ages.

Benefits of Cycling

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Strengthens leg muscles
  • Reduces stress
  • Environment-friendly

Types of Cycling

  • Road Cycling
  • Mountain Biking
  • Track Cycling
  • Recreational Cycling

Safety Tips

  • Wear a helmet
  • Follow traffic rules
  • Use reflectors and lights
  • Maintain your bicycle regularly
