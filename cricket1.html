What is Cricket?

Cricket is a popular team sport played between two teams of 11 players. The main objective is to score more runs than the opposing team.

Basic Rules

  • Each team has 11 players
  • One team bats, the other bowls and fields
  • Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running
  • Teams switch roles after innings

Cricket Equipment

  • Bat
  • Ball
  • Stumps & Bails
  • Protective Gear

Popular Formats

  • Test Cricket
  • One Day Internationals (ODI)
  • T20 Cricket

Benefits of Playing Cricket

  • Improves physical fitness
  • Builds teamwork
  • Enhances focus and coordination
  • Reduces stress
