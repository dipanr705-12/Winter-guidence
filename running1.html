What is Running?

Running is a physical activity where a person moves quickly on foot. It is one of the simplest and most effective forms of exercise.

Benefits of Running

  • Improves heart health
  • Boosts stamina and endurance
  • Helps maintain body weight
  • Reduces stress

Types of Running

  • Jogging
  • Sprinting
  • Long-distance running
  • Treadmill running

Tips for Beginners

  • Start slow and increase gradually
  • Wear comfortable shoes
  • Warm up before running
  • Stay hydrated
