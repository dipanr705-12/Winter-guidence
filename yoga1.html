What is Yoga?

Yoga is an ancient practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve overall health and mental well-being.

Benefits of Yoga

  • Improves flexibility and strength
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Enhances concentration
  • Boosts immunity

Common Yoga Types

  • Hatha Yoga
  • Vinyasa Yoga
  • Ashtanga Yoga
  • Pranayama

Tips for Beginners

  • Practice regularly
  • Start with simple poses
  • Focus on breathing
  • Stay relaxed and calm
