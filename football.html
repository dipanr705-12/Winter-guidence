What is Football?

Football (also called soccer) is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players each. The main objective is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent’s goal.

Basic Rules

  • Each team has 11 players including 1 goalkeeper.
  • Only the goalkeeper can use hands inside the penalty area.
  • A match has two halves of 45 minutes each.
  • The team with the most goals wins.

Football Field

  • Rectangular grass field
  • One goal at each end
  • Center circle, penalty areas, and sidelines

Popular Tournaments

  • FIFA World Cup
  • UEFA Champions League
  • English Premier League
  • La Liga

Benefits of Playing Football

  • Improves physical fitness
  • Builds teamwork and discipline
  • Reduces stress
  • Improves coordination and stamina
